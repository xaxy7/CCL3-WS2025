Project description

Have you ever felt ashamed that you cannot recognize a flag? You wish that you knew more about various nations around the world? Our app is exactly what you need. We allow users to quickly learn every country flag by playing a dynamic quiz game with different modes.

Team members:

Use case

The app can be used simply for learning different facts about countries all around the world. However, it can have other benefits, such as creating good habits by practicing memorisation every day. It can also serve as a fun distraction to use while bored, and one that actually teaches you some potentially useful facts, instead of brainrotting

Target user

  • Age: Teen/Young Adults, but also Seniors
  • Gender: any
  • Occupation: School and Collage Students,
  • Interests: geography, memory games, politics, daily games

Methods

This study employed a 2 round between-subject experimental design to evaluate the usability and ease of task with version A - initial and version B - improved version to evaluate usability.

A between-subject design was selected to avoid learning and transfer effects that could arise if participants interacted with multiple versions of the application.

Voluntary participants, recruited among university students

Usability Test Plan

  1. Informed Consents
  2. Demographic questioner
  3. Single Ease Score
    1. Play Flag Guessing -> Hard Mode - until you complete it or run out of lives
    2. View the summary of the completed round
    3. Select a Country Guessing mode of you choice and play until you bookmark 5 country shapes, then return to the main screen
    4. Remove one of the bookmarks on the country shape and then plan a notebook round
    5. View the History of your games from your test, and remove one of them
  4. SUS questioner
  5. Free, open feedback
